About Us

Our goal

The FightCancer is the nation's most trusted cancer treatments information. Our core purpose is advocacy, awareness and connecting people to the best resources about the rising cost of cancer treatment. Our main goal is to supply information and built a community of top doctors, hospitals, experts and survivors to help guide patients and their families.

Our free patient and family tools can help you identify the finest chemo therapists and treatment centres, access financial support, and learn more about your legal choices. We also assist veterans with VA benefits and compensation applications.

At no cost to you, our Medical Outreach team and Patient Advocates listen and respond to your questions about diagnosis and treatment. Our Doctor Match programme matches people with the finest doctors in their area based on their diagnosis and geography. We can also assist you in making an appointment.

We're dedicated to assisting you in locating the financial resources you require. We can assist you in applying for Social Security Disability benefits and locating resources such as trust funds and travel grants. We'll put you in touch with a lawyer who can help you collect the money you deserve.

Our VA-certified Claims Agents assist veterans with writing asbestos exposure narratives, gathering proof, and filing their VA papers appropriately. We also help eligible veterans who have had their claims refused file appeals so that they can receive their benefits, such as disability compensation.

Get in Touch
We would love to hear from you. Whether you are a patient looking for information on the cost of cancer, a veteran with questions about Cancer and the rise in the cost of its treatment or a reporter looking to contact our Editorial Department, we value your questions, comments and feedback.
